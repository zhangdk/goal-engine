## Prioritization Rules

Score each prospect from 0-10:

- +2 has a clear B2B/high-ticket offer.
- +2 has an obvious contact/booking path.
- +2 has founder/operator visible.
- +2 publishes content, case studies, events, or lead magnets.
- +1 uses multiple lead channels.
- +1 has signs of manual process or slow response.

Only contact prospects scoring 7+.
